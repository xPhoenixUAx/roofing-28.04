2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer, smartphone, tablet, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to ensure websites function properly, improve performance, and provide website owners with useful operational information.

Cookies may be:

  • Session Cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies — cookies that remain on your device for a defined period or until manually deleted.
  • First-Party Cookies — cookies placed directly by our website.
  • Third-Party Cookies — cookies placed by external services integrated into our website.

5. How to Manage Cookies

You have the right to accept, reject, or manage cookies.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to disable or remove cookies if preferred.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Most major browsers provide cookie controls, including:
