About the platform

Helping Homeowners Find Roofing Help With More Clarity

RoofBridge Connect is not a roofing contractor. It is a homeowner assistance and referral platform that helps users connect with independent local providers.

Platform story

Roofing problems can feel urgent, expensive, and hard to sort through.

Homeowners often notice a leak, storm concern, aging roof, or new construction need before they know who to call. RoofBridge Connect exists to help organize the request, explain common roofing paths, and connect homeowners with relevant independent providers when available.

The goal is speed with clarity: collect useful details, route the request, and encourage informed decisions before any hiring conversation moves forward.

What the platform does

Built to make the first step more organized.

RoofBridge Connect gives homeowners a focused way to begin a roofing search without implying that one provider is automatically the right choice.

Collect request details

Forms ask for contact information, ZIP code, service need, preferred contact method, and project notes.

Route homeowners to providers

Requests may be routed to independent local providers based on location, project type, and availability.

Provide educational guidance

Service pages explain common roofing concerns, warning signs, and questions homeowners may want to ask.

Encourage verification

Homeowners are reminded to verify licensing, insurance, written scope, warranty terms, and provider qualifications.

Important distinction

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not perform roofing work.
  • We do not employ roofing crews.
  • We do not guarantee contractor performance.
  • We do not warrant or guarantee any work performed.
  • We do not replace the homeowner's responsibility to verify license and insurance.
Referral model

Independent providers, homeowner decisions.

Any inspection, estimate, price, timeline, warranty, or project agreement is between the homeowner and the independent provider. RoofBridge Connect helps with the connection process and educational context, not the physical roofing work.

Service availability may vary by location, project type, season, weather, and provider capacity.
