Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how RoofBridge Connect collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information.

Last updated: January 2026
Privacy PolicyTerms of ServiceCookie Policy

1. Introduction

RoofBridge Connect ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website or use our services.

RoofBridge Connect is a local roofing-focused service helping organize residential roof repair, replacement, inspections, maintenance, and emergency roofing requests with more clarity. RoofBridge Connect is not a roofing contractor and does not directly perform roofing work.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • submit contact forms
  • request roofing assistance
  • request inspections or estimates
  • communicate with us by phone or email
  • subscribe to updates where applicable
  • participate in surveys or feedback requests

This information may include:

  • full name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • property address
  • mailing address
  • roofing project details
  • storm damage information where relevant
  • any additional information you choose to provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • approximate geographic location
  • browser type and version
  • operating system
  • device information
  • pages visited
  • time spent on pages
  • referring websites
  • technical usage data and interaction patterns

This information helps us improve website functionality and service performance.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar technologies to collect browsing information and improve user experience.

These technologies help us with:

  • website functionality
  • analytics
  • performance monitoring
  • marketing measurement
  • user preference storage

For more details, please review our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for purposes including:

  • responding to service requests
  • coordinating roofing-related assistance and provider connections
  • helping route requests for inspections and estimates where available
  • customer support
  • service planning and project communication
  • improving website functionality
  • marketing communications where consent is provided
  • analytics and performance review
  • fraud prevention and security
  • legal compliance and operational requirements

We only use personal data where there is a legitimate business or legal reason to do so.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

We may share information only where necessary with:

  • independent roofing providers and operational partners
  • payment processors where applicable
  • scheduling and communication systems
  • analytics providers
  • legal authorities where required by law
  • professional advisors during legitimate business operations
  • business transfer scenarios such as merger, acquisition, or restructuring

All relevant third parties are expected to handle information securely and responsibly. Independent providers may maintain their own privacy practices and policies.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information.

These may include:

  • SSL encryption
  • secure hosting environments
  • access controls
  • security monitoring
  • internal process controls
  • regular security reviews where applicable

However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Users submit information at their own reasonable risk.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • access to your data
  • correction of inaccurate information
  • deletion of personal information
  • restriction of processing
  • objection to certain processing activities
  • withdrawal of marketing consent
  • data portability where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps to remove such data.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or external service providers.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of those third-party websites.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

9. California Privacy Rights

Residents of California may have additional privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and related laws.

These rights may include:

  • the right to know what personal data is collected
  • the right to request deletion
  • the right to request disclosure of information categories
  • the right to opt out of certain qualifying data sales where applicable
  • protection from discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States.

By using our website, you acknowledge and consent to such transfers where permitted by applicable law.

We take reasonable measures to ensure transferred data remains appropriately protected.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • fulfill service obligations or request coordination purposes
  • comply with legal requirements
  • resolve disputes
  • enforce agreements
  • maintain operational records

When data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted, anonymized, or otherwise disposed of appropriately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal, operational, or technical changes.

Updates will be published on this page along with a revised "Last Updated" date.
