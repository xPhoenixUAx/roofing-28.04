Reusable generation rules:
Use realistic professional photography for the USA residential roofing market. Show real homes, asphalt shingles, metal roofing where relevant, ladders, tools, safety gear, and natural daylight. Avoid logos, readable brand names, futuristic elements, neon lighting, exaggerated damage, robots, cyber style, and unrealistic roof shapes. Images should feel trustworthy, local, clean, and premium.
